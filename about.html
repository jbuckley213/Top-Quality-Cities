Where is the data from?

Looking to move to a new city but not sure how to chose? Why not let data make the decision for you! Top Quality Cities lets you search for any major city in the world and will show you the life quality data for that area. As well as a overrall score Top Quality Cities will provide the individual rating for Housing, Startups, Tolerance, Environmental Quality and many more. Find your new city today!
